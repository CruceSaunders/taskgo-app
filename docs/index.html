TaskGo!

A tranquil menu bar productivity app for macOS. Stay focused, earn XP, level up your day.

Download for macOS v1.0.0

Requires macOS 14.0 (Sonoma) or later

Everything you need, nothing you don't

⚡

Task Go Mode

A floating timer counts down your task. When time's up, it gently bounces and plays a peaceful alarm. Complete or add more time — your call.

⭐

XP & Levels

Earn 1 XP for every minute of verified work. Level up to 100 with progressively harder milestones. Your productivity, gamified.

📄

Smart Task Groups

Organize tasks into named groups with tabs. "Urgent", "Side Projects", "Errands" — whatever works for your brain.

🕒

Time Estimates

Set how long each task should take. TaskGo! uses this to run your focus sessions and keep you honest about time.

💻

Lives in Your Menu Bar

One click to open, one click to add a task. No dock icon, no window management. It's just there when you need it.

🔒

Privacy-First Activity Check

XP verification detects keyboard and mouse activity — never what you type. Your work is verified, your privacy is protected.

How it works

1

Add your tasks

Click the menu bar icon, add tasks with names, descriptions, and time estimates. Organize them into groups.

2

Hit "Task Go!"

A floating timer appears on screen. It starts counting down from your first task's time estimate.

3

Work through your list

Complete tasks, earn XP, and watch yourself level up. The timer auto-advances to the next task when you're done.

4

Feel accomplished

See your level grow, track your weekly XP, and know exactly how productive you were.

Gamify your grind

TaskGo! turns your daily work into a progression system. Every minute of real work earns you XP. How high can you level?
